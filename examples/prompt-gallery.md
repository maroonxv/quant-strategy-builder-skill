# Prompt Gallery

These prompts are written for direct reuse in Codex-style tools. Replace framework and asset details to match your repo.

## Generic Research

- `Use $quant-strategy-builder to turn my idea for a US equity mean-reversion strategy into a concrete plan with signal rules, exit logic, risk limits, and the smallest useful backtest.`
- `Use $quant-strategy-builder to review this strategy repo and tell me whether the logic for universe, signal, sizing, and execution is cleanly separated or mixed together.`
- `Use $quant-strategy-builder to port this breakout strategy from a notebook into maintainable strategy code with explicit tests and validation steps.`

## LEAN / QuantConnect

- `Use $quant-strategy-builder to implement a LEAN trend-following strategy with scheduled rebalances, warm-up handling, and realistic fee and slippage assumptions.`
- `Use $quant-strategy-builder to add an options wheel strategy to this LEAN project, including assignment transitions, capital limits, and backtest evidence.`

## Freqtrade

- `Use $quant-strategy-builder to improve this Freqtrade strategy by cleaning up duplicate indicators, tightening protections, and validating the effect with a focused backtest.`
- `Use $quant-strategy-builder to add informative pairs and regime filters to this Freqtrade strategy without making the entry and exit logic harder to maintain.`

## Backtrader

- `Use $quant-strategy-builder to refactor this Backtrader strategy so signal logic, sizing, and analyzers are separated cleanly and verified with explicit analyzer output.`
- `Use $quant-strategy-builder to add a volatility-targeting position sizer to this Backtrader setup and show the minimum test and backtest evidence needed.`

## OptionForge

- `Use $quant-strategy-builder to modify this OptionForge strategy while staying inside the active editable surface and reporting validate, focus test, and backtest evidence.`
- `Use $quant-strategy-builder to map this options idea onto the right OptionForge config, domain-service, and test surfaces before making edits.`
